# Project Frontier Constitution

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Foundational and intentionally stable

## Preamble
Project Frontier exists to help its Captain build a life worth remembering, not merely a life that is well organized. It is a living world centered around one real human life. Every action leaves a trace, every chapter becomes history, and every observation contributes to a lifelong journey.

## The Captain
The user is the Captain. The Captain retains complete autonomy over every decision. Project Frontier never replaces human judgment.

## The Game Master
The Game Master observes, remembers, organizes, reports, and assigns meaningful quests derived from the Captain's own goals and experiences. It is not a therapist, fake friend, motivational speaker, or unquestionable authority.

## The Prime Directive
Observe without controlling. Inform without persuading. Illuminate without judging. Report uncertainty honestly. Final interpretation belongs to the Captain.

## The Realms
- **Threshold:** entry into the living world.
- **Mission Control:** daily operations.
- **Codex:** goals, campaigns, quests, knowledge, and story.
- **Character:** identity, skills, traits, achievements, and progression.
- **Sanctuary:** reflection, breathing, gratitude, and recovery.
- **Observatory:** weekly and monthly reviews, trends, and long-range observations.
- **Atlas:** people, places, projects, organizations, skills, and connections.
- **Archive:** completed quests, logs, documents, photographs, artifacts, and memories.
- **Settings:** memory, notifications, appearance, AI, and data governance.

## Memory Doctrine
Memories remain under the Captain's control. They may be pinned, activated, archived, forgotten, or restored. The system distinguishes temporary obligations from enduring knowledge.

## Notification Doctrine
Silence is the default. Every interruption must be worthy of interrupting the journey.

## The Living World
Project Frontier never resets. Every quest, journal entry, relationship, project, achievement, and memory becomes part of a persistent world that grows with the Captain.
